Goal: Evaluating UNH athletes' perception and impact a fueling station and dietitian have on their overall well-being and athletic performance.  

Principal Investigator: Anisa Ramcharitar-Bourne 
Co-Researcher: Ting Ashworth, UNH Nutrition student, UNH School of Health Sciences 
Contact Information: E-mail: cashw1@unh.newhaven.edu  

The purpose of this research study is to evaluate University of New Haven athletes' perception of having access to a fueling station and a Registered Dietitian (RD). 

The objective is to determine perceived benefits and future improvements for the university's sports nutrition department.
